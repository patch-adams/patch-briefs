## References

### 2026-01-31

#### Firewatch Game Website
- **URL**: https://www.firewatchgame.com/
- **Brief**: Greg (Signal Form Vision)
- **Designer**: Olly Moss / Campo Santo
- **Key Elements**:
  - 1960s National Park poster aesthetic
  - Layered parallax silhouettes
  - Warm sunset palette (oranges, deep reds, golden yellows)
  - Typography: bold, minimal, poster-like
  - Cohesive identity from game to website to box art
- **Source**: [Olly Moss Portfolio](http://ollymoss.com/firewatch), [Parallax Recreation Tutorial](https://medium.com/@hamstu/recreating-the-firewatch-parallax-effect-213694d42f4e)

#### Odyssey Works
- **URL**: https://www.odysseyworks.org/
- **Brief**: Greg (Signal Form Vision)
- **Key Elements**:
  - Transformative experience design
  - Immersive, personalized experiences
  - Empathy-driven methodology
  - Worldbuilding and storytelling focus
  - The "unknown" as defining quality of liveness
- **Philosophy**: Experience Design is discipline-agnostic; the final product is the experience itself, not the artifact
